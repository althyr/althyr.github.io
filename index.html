My style as a director is naturalistic, hence my affinity with Scandinavian and European playwrights. I am committed to revealing real people in an extract of their lives, which resemble the audience's most private moments.

Brazilian creative based in London. Trained at the Giles Foreman Centre for Acting in Stanislavski and Movement Psychology (Yat Malmgren lineage). Theatre work at the White Bear Theatre, Bridewell Theatre and Southwark Playhouse. Four plays translated from Portuguese. 20+ titles dubbed for Netflix Brazil. Available for directing, acting, translation and audio work across London and Europe.
